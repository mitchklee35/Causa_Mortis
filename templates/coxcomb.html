Coxcomb Diagram:

The coxcomb diagram or sometimes referred to as polar area charts or roses is best used when trying to visualize patterns rather than delivering exact numbers. We can observe that over the period from 1999 - 2017 that the leading cause of death among Americans is Heart Disease and Cancer. Understanding this trend allows public and private institutions to shape policy or implement business strategies that will better serve their customers and constituents. Specifically, doctors could recommend to patients dietary changes and setting up regular screenings for cancer.
